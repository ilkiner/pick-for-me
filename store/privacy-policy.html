Pick For Me

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026  ·  Effective: June 2026

Short version: Pick For Me stores your data locally on your device. If you create an account, your lists and history are synced to our secure servers. We do not sell your data. Ads are served by Google AdMob — see below.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the minimum data needed to provide the service:

  • Account data (if you register): email address and encrypted password, stored in Supabase with industry-standard security.
  • Saved lists and history: the content you create in the app (wheel options, saved lists, activity history). Stored locally and, if you're signed in, synced to our Supabase database.
  • Purchase information: subscription status managed by RevenueCat. We never store raw payment details — those stay with Apple/Google.
  • Analytics data (anonymous): crash reports and usage metrics via Expo's built-in telemetry. No personally identifiable information.

2. Advertising (Google AdMob)

The free version of Pick For Me displays ads served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect the following to serve relevant ads:

  • Device advertising identifier (IDFA on iOS, GAID on Android)
  • Approximate location (country/region level only)
  • App usage behavior

On iOS, we ask for your permission via the App Tracking Transparency prompt before accessing your IDFA. You can withdraw consent at any time in your device Settings.

Pro subscribers see zero ads. Upgrading to Pick For Me Pro fully disables ad tracking and ad display.

Google's privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and sync the core app features (saved lists, history)
  • To manage your subscription through RevenueCat
  • To display relevant ads to free users
  • To diagnose crashes and improve the app

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing.

4. Data Storage and Security

Local data is stored on your device using AsyncStorage and SecureStore (encrypted). Cloud data is stored in Supabase (hosted on AWS) with row-level security — only you can access your own data.

Auth tokens are stored in SecureStore (iOS Keychain / Android Keystore), protected by your device's secure enclave.

5. Data Retention

  • Activity history: retained for 48 hours locally; cloud history kept until you delete it.
  • Saved lists: kept until you delete them.
  • Account data: kept until you delete your account.

To delete your account and all associated data, email us at pickforme.app@gmail.com with the subject "Delete my account".

6. Children's Privacy

Pick For Me is rated Everyone. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us personal information, please contact us immediately.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data (right to be forgotten)
  • Opt out of ad tracking (iOS: App Tracking Transparency; Android: device settings)

8. Third-Party Services

  • Supabase — database and auth: supabase.com/privacy
  • RevenueCat — subscription management: revenuecat.com/privacy
  • Google AdMob — advertising: policies.google.com/privacy
  • Expo / EAS — build and OTA updates: expo.dev/privacy

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes in the app or legal requirements. Significant changes will be communicated via an in-app notice. The "Last updated" date at the top will always reflect the most recent revision.
