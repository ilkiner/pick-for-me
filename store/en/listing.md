## Full Description (max 4000 chars — Google Play)

**Can't decide? Pick For Me decides for you.**

Pick For Me is the all-in-one decision tool that replaces a dozen separate apps.
Clean dark interface, zero clutter, works completely offline.

**🎡 Wheel of Fortune**
Spin a fully customizable wheel — add your own options, save them as lists,
reuse them anytime. No ads during spins.

**🎲 Dice Roller**
1 or 2 dice with satisfying animations. Roll to settle any argument instantly.

**🪙 Coin Flip**
Heads or tails with a realistic flip animation. Even has a rare edge result.

**🎨 Color Picker**
5 color palettes (Organic, Vivid, Digital, Soft, Random). Copies HEX, saves
your palette history. Perfect for design decisions.

**💡 Random Idea**
Stuck? Get a random activity suggestion filtered by mood — Relaxed, Productive,
Fun, or Social. Add your own ideas.

**⚡ Quick Challenge**
A small, instant action to break your routine. Do it now, skip it later.

**🎬 Movie Picker**
Can't agree on what to watch? Add movies, pick from curated genre lists, or
let the app decide. All-Time Classics & Trending Picks categories included.

**🏆 Tournament Bracket**
Enter your contenders, pick winners round by round, crown a champion. Works
for anything — movies, restaurants, travel destinations.

**👥 Order & Teams**
Shuffle a list into a random order, or split people into balanced random teams.
Perfect for games, classrooms, and group activities.

**📋 My Lists**
Save and reuse your custom lists across all tools. Create once, use everywhere.

---

**Why Pick For Me over the rest?**
✔ Everything in one app — no more switching
✔ Fully offline — no internet required, ever
✔ Clean dark UI — easy on your eyes
✔ Saves your lists — never retype the same options
✔ Honest monetization — light ads, clear Pro pricing, no tricks

**Pick For Me Pro** removes all ads and unlocks unlimited saved lists, extended
history, and premium features. Monthly and yearly plans available with a free trial.
