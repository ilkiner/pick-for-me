Account & Data Deletion

Hesap ve Veri Silme Talebi

Deleting your account removes: your login credentials, saved lists, and activity history from our servers. This action is permanent and cannot be undone.

Option 1 — Delete from within the app (instant)

  1. Open Pick For Me
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Tap Delete Account (at the bottom)
  4. Confirm twice — your account and all data are deleted immediately

Option 2 — Request deletion by email

If you no longer have the app installed, send us a deletion request from the email address associated with your account:

Request Account Deletion

We process email requests within 30 days. You will receive a confirmation once your account and data have been removed.

What is deleted

  • Account credentials (email, hashed password)
  • Saved lists synced to the cloud
  • Activity history synced to the cloud

Data stored only on your device (local history, preferences) is removed when you uninstall the app. Anonymized analytics that cannot be linked to you may be retained as described in our Privacy Policy.

Türkçe — Hesap Silme

Uygulama içinden (anında): Pick For Me → Ayarlar → en alttaki Hesabı Sil → iki kez onayla. Hesabın ve tüm verilerin anında silinir.

E-posta ile: Hesabına bağlı e-posta adresinden pickforme.app@gmail.com adresine "Hesap Silme Talebi" konulu bir e-posta gönder. Talepler en geç 30 gün içinde işlenir.

Silinenler: hesap bilgileri, buluta kaydedilmiş listeler ve aktivite geçmişi. Bu işlem geri alınamaz.
